Attending university or college, students have to deal with a lot of things together such as studying for exams, finishing assignments on time, attending lectures, working on projects, etc. Managing all of the tasks, students are always in rush.

Student Life News is a Web Service that offers students useful information about important events, weather forecasts, and even the probability of there being a snow day on a given date. The services are all available through Student Life News's REST API.
